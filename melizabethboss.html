Main Team Key Units Analysis

Unit Remark

Gowther		 Gowther is mainly to be used for ranking Escanor/Merlin/Jericho's skills. If you are able to get an early ultimate ready, you will be able to delay Elizabeth's ultimate skill's heal in Phase 1.
Escanor will be able to get his ultimate fast when paired with Gowther. Since there are a lot of debuffing going on in both phase 1 and phase 2, the immunity to debuffs when he has MAX ult gauge helps to alleviate the burden a little. Aside from that, his ultimate skill also hits super hard combined with a rank 3 Merlin freeze.
Merlin's role in this team is to provide gold freezes so the nukers will be able to dish out damage. Her attack card sealing on the ultimate skill can also stop Elizabeth from attacking. If you have her ultimate leveled up to above 4/6, a decently geared Merlin can also act as a nuker. For players who do not have Merlin, a budget alternative is Gustaf, who has the same skill sets as Merlin aside from the ultimate skill and passive.
Jericho is also a top tier DPS when it comes to the Super Boss, however, she has slightly higher gear requirements compared to Escanor, and would ideally work best when she has a 6/6 ultimate and have 3x CDMG gear or ATK/CDMG. She has innate lifesteal so when combined with lifesteal food, she is self sustainable.

Sub Units Analysis

Unit Remark
Alioni's passive boosts the ATK-related stats of Vitality attribute units such as Escanor and Jericho by 10%. In the ideal situation, Alioni should not be present on the field to "fight". A minor downside of using Alioni on the sub slot is that he has relatively low CP, without investing heavily into Alioni himself or having a spare set of decent gear, you might be having trouble to hit over 160k CP requirements to start first in HELL.
As the alternative to Alioni, Howzer has a lot more base CP than Alioni. He provides 8% ATK, DEF and HP boost to human characters. He provides less in terms of ATK boost to the main DPS (Escanor, Jericho), but he does give them a little more survivability in terms of HP and DEF. If you have trouble reaching 160k CP with Alioni, Howzer is the next best option.

Strategies and Tips + FAQ

● Aim for Meliodas in Phase 1 first before moving onto Elizabeth.

● Phase 1's Meliodas takes 50% more damage in the counter stance, and he WILL NOT counterattack if you are using attacking cards (non-debuff cards). If you are running Escanor, you can still use Escanor's Skill 1 on Meliodas.

● When Phase 1 Elizabeth gets off her ultimate, the initial heal isn't going to heal much. The annoying part is the Cure that comes with the ult, which is a Heal-over-Time. When Cure is present on a decently low health Meliodas and Elizabeth, you might want to consider investing a rank 1 freeze to take it away.

● Ideally you would want to save at least 1 ult of main DPS or Merlin and 1 gold freeze going into Phase 2, so you can immediately nuke down Elizabeth in Phase 2 turn 1.

● Since Meliodas does not counter attacking skills in Phase 1, it is NOT necessary to freeze him before attacking him every single time.

● Both Meliodas and Elizabeth in both Phase 1 and Phase 2 are immune to Corrosion, Petrification and Stuns.
However, Meliodas in both Phases cleanse freezes at the start of his turn. On the other hand, Elizabeth does not cleanse the freeze - she is susceptible to freezes.
